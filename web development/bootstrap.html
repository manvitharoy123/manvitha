...Venice...

Venice, the capital of northern Italy’s Veneto region, is built on more than 100 small islands in a lagoon in the Adriatic Sea. It has no roads, just canals – including the Grand Canal thoroughfare – lined with Renaissance and Gothic palaces.

1)It’s difficult to describe Venice in a word or two. It is beautiful, and that is probably an understatement. It is absorbing. And it is most definitely unique

2)The city exudes confidence. It is a series of islands and so is at risk of flooding, but that has never stopped the Venetians building the most spectacular buildings and structures here.
